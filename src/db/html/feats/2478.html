Point Blank Shot

( Player's Handbook v.3.5, p. 98)

[Fighter Bonus Feat, General]

You are skilled at making well-placed shots with ranged weapons at close range.

Prerequisite

Required for

Aquatic Shot (Sto) , Cobolt Precision (MoI) , Combat Archery (CW) , Combat Archery (EL) , Coordinated Shot (HB) , Deadeye Shot (PH2) , Defensive Archery (RW) , Distant Shot (EL) , Far Shot (PH) , Fell Shot (PsH3_) , Fell Shot (XPH) , Greater Manyshot (XPH) , Greater Psionic Shot (XPH) , Improved Arrow of Death (EL) , Improved Manyshot (EL) , Improved Precise Shot (PH) , Improved Rapid Shot (CW) , Manyshot (EL) , Manyshot (PH) , Penetrating Shot (PH2) , Precise Shot (PH) , Psionic Shot (PsH3_) , Psionic Shot (XPH) , Ranged Disarm (CW) , Ranged Pin (CW) , Ranged Recall (CM) , Ranged Sunder (CW) , Rapid Shot (PH) , Ready Shot (HB) , Return Shot (PsH3_) , Return Shot (XPH) , Sharp-Shooting (CW) , Sharp-Shooting (DD) , Sharp-Shooting (SF) , Shot on the Run (PH) , Storm of Throws (EL) , Swarm of Arrows (EL) , Uncanny Accuracy (EL) , Woodland Archer (RW) , Dead Eye (DC) , Tormtor School (DrU) , Xaniqos School (DrU) , Plunging Shot (HB) , Plunging Shot (RW) ,

Benefit

You get a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls with ranged weapons at ranges of up to 30 feet.

Special

A fighter may select Point Blank Shot as one of his fighter bonus feats (see page 38).
